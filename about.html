Who are King's Head Fc

Kings Head FC were formed back in July 2022, after signing up for Warrington Sunday League we were looking for sponsors to help fund the clubs kit, pitch etc. This is where the Kingshead pub came along and offered to help the team if we changed the teams name. Since then, KingsHead FC has been competing in Warrington's Sunday League.

The Kingshead pub

The Kingshead pub is a local pub that has help create this team, located at 40 Winwick Street, Warrington WA2 7TU this is where players can come back enjoy free food the pub provide and watch any football games that may be on later on the day. Most of the lads will like to come back for one or two drinks whilst watching Premier League games and talking about the result of the game they just played.

Where we play and train

Most of the time the lads will train at Orford Jubliee on a Thursday between 7-8pm. Games are played on a Sunday with kick off being at 11 however there could be some games kicking off midweek with the games being played on a Wednesday.

What we need from you!

We accept any ability in any position all we ask is that you keep up to date with subs, give 100% on gameday and to come back to the pub and help support the pub that gave this team the opportunity to play.
