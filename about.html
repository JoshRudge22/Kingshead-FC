Who are King's Head Fc

Kings Head FC were formed back in July 2022, after signing up for Warrington Sunday League we were looking for sponsors to help fund the clubs kit, picth ect. This is wheere the Kingshead pub came along and offered to help the team as long as we changed the team name. Since then Kings Head FC has been competing in Warrington's Sunday League.
